About

What is a Mask Bloc?

Mask Blocs are volunteer-run mutual-aid groups that distribute free high-filtration masks (N95/KN95/FFP2), COVID-19 tests, and clean-air tools to their communities. No cost, no questions asked. They’re grounded in disability justice and the simple idea that we keep us safe. This directory exists so people can find the bloc nearest them, and so blocs can find each other.

This is a community project and a successor in spirit to the original maskbloc.org. Listings are submitted by the public and reviewed by a human before publishing. To suggest an edit or removal, use the form above or get in touch on social media.
